MAYA HLADISOVA

Maya Hladisova is an Environmental Design student at CU Boulder emphasizing in Architecture with a certificate in Lighting Design. She strives to make lasting designs that feel integrated with the surrounding environment and bring people satisfaction. Passionate about ecological values, she aims to produce environmentally conscious solutions without compromising comfort. Whether it is designing a space catered to help a client achieve their ideal lifestyle, or creating a sense of peace in dense urban locations, Maya values the human experience above all. 
In her free time, she loves experimenting with cooking, singing, and exploring the world.
